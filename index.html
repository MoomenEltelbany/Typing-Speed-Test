    Game instructions

  1. You have three levels for testing how fast you type.
  2. Each difficulty has a different set of words to measure your typing speed.
  3. Each difficulty has a different timing interval for you to write your word.
  4. For the first time and only the first time, you will gain extra three seconds.
  5. To pass, you have to write every word correctly.
  6. If you fail to write the word correctly, you will have to restart all over again.
  7. No need to worry about writing the words in capital or small words.
  8. At the end of each game you can find your score at each level, how many times you and how many you lost
